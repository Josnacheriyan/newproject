About Al Shihab Al Thahabi

Over the years Al Shihab Al Thahabi has built an impeccable reputation as the preferred supplier of chemicals and equipment for all types of industries. Something our competitors cannot match in terms of experience and expertise. With our strategic location in Sharjah, UAE we have exceptionally strong trading ties across the GCC, Sudan, Ethiopia and Eritrea are able to provide overnight delivery of material and equipment to most locations.

We pride ourselves in being a single point solution for all your requirements whether they are additives for food industry, chemicals for construction, coatings, polymer industry or plastic processing machinery. Our products touch every facet of modern life.

We strive to surpass your expectations at every step of the way so you always get speciality chemicals that you need, exactly when you need them. We take pride in providing the highest quality products available anywhere in the market at extremely competitive prices so that our customers enjoy total value for money.

With our state of the art warehouse facilities we offer material handling expertise and logistical capabilities to meet every need. Whether it is food ingredients, speciality chemicals, liquid or solid, drummed or bagged, with Al Shihab Al Thahabi you can be assured of safe, professional and skilled handling. And that’s not all. We have a team of qualified chemical engineers and food technologists who are able to help you with expert solutions to all your needs.

When it comes to our long relationship with our customers, many believe the secret is that we have the right ‘chemistry’. After all, we leverage our knowledge, experience and expertise to deliver outstanding support to our valued clients, no matter what the challenge.

People often are fascinated by our name and ask about its meaning. In Arabic ‘Shihab’ means a shooting star and ‘Thahabi’, is Arabic for ‘Golden’. So literally a golden shooting star.

Our Mission

To build on our leadership position with unwavering commitment to the highest quality, best-in-class customer service combined with impeccable ethics. We are driven by our passion to serve our customers through teamwork, innovation and expertise, and build long lasting relationships based on these values.
